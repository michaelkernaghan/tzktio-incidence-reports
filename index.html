Incident Report 1: Discrepancy in Missed Rewards Reporting

Reported By: Michael Kernaghan

Platform: Parisnet (Tezos Blockchain Explorer)

Date Reported: May 1, 2024

Description of the Issue

A discrepancy has been observed in the rewards history reporting on the Parisnet interface for the user identified by the address tz1M6txwzVsUU9DCd2odaHnsc38spSHycX5r. The "Rewards History" section for cycle 26 reported a missed reward of approximately 19,655.33 ꜩ, whereas the "Operations" section reported a much larger figure of approximately 288,007.90 ꜩ missed as endorsing rewards.

Analysis

The operations screen indicates a significantly higher missed reward compared to the detailed cycle-based rewards history. This might be due to a frontend instability, causing inaccurate numbers to be displayed.
